Can customers place orders directly on the website now?

Yes. They can add products to bag, submit delivery details, and choose a payment method during checkout.

Paystack payments can verify automatically, while manual mobile money and bank transfer can still be confirmed from the admin dashboard.

How are new products added?

Staff can use the admin dashboard to create new items with price, stock, description, and category.
